#+title: Grice's Maxims

* Maxim of Quantity
Be as informative as possible, but only as informative as needed.
* Maxim of Quality
Be truthful / evidence-based.
* Maxim of Relation
Be relevant.
* Maxim of Manner
Be clear, concise, and orderly.
